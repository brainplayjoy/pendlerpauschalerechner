Deductible Allowance

€0.00

How does the distance allowance work?

  • For the first 20 kilometers: €0.30 per kilometer.
  • From the 21st kilometer: Increased rate of €0.38 per kilometer.
  • Important: If you use public transport (bus, train, bicycle, etc.), the allowance is capped at €4,500 per year. This limit does not apply to trips made with your own car.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Does the allowance apply to the round trip?

No, the commuter allowance applies exclusively to the one-way distance.

What happens on home office days?

No distance allowance can be claimed for home office days. You can use the home office allowance for this instead.
